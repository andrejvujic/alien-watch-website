Privacy Policy

Last updated on December 19, 2024.

Introduction

Thank you for using Alien Watch (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application (“the App”), available on Google Play. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to improve the user experience and provide better services. These tools help us track app usage, diagnose issues, and monitor performance.

  • Firebase Analytics collects data on user interactions within the App to understand how users engage with the App, which helps us improve functionality and content.
  • Firebase Crashlytics collects crash reports and error logs to monitor and fix issues, ensuring that we can quickly resolve problems that affect your experience with the App.

2. Types of Data Collected

The data we collect is limited to the following:

  • Crash Reports: When the App encounters an error, Crashlytics collects information about the crash, including the device type, operating system version, app version, and any relevant error messages.
  • App Usage Data: Firebase Analytics collects data on how you use the App, including interactions with various features and the frequency of usage. This helps us understand which features are most valuable to you and identify areas for improvement.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data collected to:

  • Fix Issues: Firebase Crashlytics helps us monitor crashes and errors, enabling us to fix them quickly and improve the App’s performance.
  • Enhance User Experience: Firebase Analytics provides insights into how users interact with the App, helping us improve its features and functionality based on user behavior.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as described in this policy. Firebase services may process your data on our behalf to help us improve the App’s performance and stability.

Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics are provided by Google. For more information about how Google handles data, please refer to their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Retention

The data collected by Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Crash reports and error logs are retained for a period determined by Firebase and may be deleted once the issue is resolved.

6. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the data collected by the App. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can opt out of Firebase Analytics by disabling tracking in your device settings or by using the App’s settings to limit data collection.
  • Control Crash Reporting: You can choose to disable crash reporting in the App settings if you do not wish to share crash data.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document, and the updated date will be indicated at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us.
