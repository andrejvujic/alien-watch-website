Privacy Policy

Omnitrix doesn't collect any user data.

User data collection isn't even possible for us because we don't have a place to store the data as we don't have a database. We don't own a server where the database could be hosted. Even this website relies on a 3rd party service, Firebase Hosting, to be online.

No analytics software, service or tool like Google Analytics is being used in the app and we have no insight in any information about the users of our app.

The app itself is completely static and there isn't any form of user-generated content, so there isn't really anything about the users of our app which we could keep track of and store.
