Prototype

Prototype mode is the deafult mode. You can return to Prototype mode while in Ultimatrix mode by using the Ultimatrix mode activation sequence.

In Prototype mode you have access to the 10 original aliens by default. The default transformation and cooldown durations are 30 seconds, but can be customized using the Alien Watch Companion app for Android or using the Preferences Editor on your Wear OS device.

Prototype mode contains two other sub-modes:

  • Master Control
  • Self Destruction
  • Virtual Reality transformations

Prototype Master Control

Activation sequence: L, R, L, R, L, R, L, R, L, R.

In Master Control mode you gain access to a total of 55 aliens.

The transformations are infinite, meaning that you can stay transformed how long you want! Cooldowns don't require you wait before being able to transform again, meaning that you can exit cooldown whenever you like.

There's also a quick transformation feature which you can use by long pressing the screen when your Omnitrix is locked.

Prototype Self Destruction

Activation sequence: L, L, R, R, L, L, R, R, L, L.

There are multiple phases in the Self Destruction sequence. After all of the phases are completed your Omnitrix will be explode! To cancel the Self Destruction you can use the same sequence you used to activate it.

Prototype Fusion transformations

Activation sequence: L, R, L, R, R, L, R, L, R, R

After using the activation code you will first go through the activation animation. After the animation finishes you can click the center of the screen to perform the transformation. Just make sure that you attempt to perform the transformation when there's no electricity on the screen!

To exit Fusion mode long press the screen in stead of clicking.

All aliens seen in Dr. Animo and the Mutant Ray are available: Heat jaws (Heatblast + Rip jaws), Diamond matter (Diamondhead + Grey matter) and Stink arms (Stinkfly + Four arms).

Virtual Reality transformations

Not all smartphones support VR. Google Play Services for AR must be supported on your smartphone!

Virtual Reality transformation are available only when in Master Control mode. You also need to have the Alien Watch Companion app for Android on your smartphone. Remember that your smartphone and smartwatch need to be paired!

Aliens which support Virtual Reality trasnformations will be shown inside a 3D/VR environment on your smartphone after transforming into them on your smartwatch!

Aliens which support Virtual Reality transformations: Heatblast, XLR8, Diamondhead, Grey Matter, Four Arms, Upgrade, Ghostfreak, Cannonbolt, Wildvine, Echo Echo, Alien X.

Ultimatrix

Activation sequence: L, L, L, R, L, R, L, R, L, R.

You can activate Ultimatrix by using it's activation sequence when in Prototype mode. You can also return to Ultimatrix mode from Omniverse mode by using the Omniverse mode activation sequence.

In Ultimatrix mode you have access to a total of 34 aliens. You can perform ultimate transformations with 9 of those aliens, while 22 scream their name when performing a normal transformation.

Aliens which currently scream their name when transforming: Swampfire, Humungousaur, Spidermonkey, Big Chill, Jetray, Ghostfreak, Chromastone, Brainstorm, Way Big, Alien X, Cannonbolt, Diamondhead, Terraspin, Armodrillo, NRG, Ampfibian, Ghostfreak, Chamalien, Eatle, Fasttrack, Clockwork, Juryrigg.

You can disable the sound effects of aliens screaming their voices using the Preferences Editor.

Ultimatrix mode contains three other sub-modes:

  • Master Control
  • Self Destruction
  • Ultimate Trasnformations (Evolved Forms)

Ultimatrix Master Control

Activation sequence: L, R, L, R, L, R, L, R, L, R.

Master Control enables quick transformations and ultimate transformations. To perform quick transformations you need to long press the screen when the Ultimatrix is locked. Read more about ultimate transformations below.

Unlike Prototype mode where the Master Control state is persisent in Ultimatrix mode you need to activate Master Control every time you open the app.

Azmuth Easter Egg

Ultimatrix Master Control contains a little easter egg. When you attempt to activate Master Control there's a 20% chance that the activation will fail. If that happens Azmuth will appear on the screen warning you about messing with the Omnitrix.

Ultimatrix Self Destruction

Activation sequence: L, L, R, R, L, L, R, R, L, L.

There are multiple phases in the Self Destruction sequence. The complete sequence lasts about 30 seconds not including the activation animation. After all of the phases are completed your Omnitrix will be explode!

In the Ultimatrix Self Destruction sequence there's also a countdown. The Ultimatrix speaks to let you know how much time's left before the detonation.

To cancel the Self Destruction you can use the same sequence you used to activate it. But, make sure you cancel on time because you can't abort if there's less than five seconds on the countdown.

Ultimate Transformations

Ultimate transformations are available only when Master Control is enabled.

After performing the regular transformation instead of clicking the screen, to go into cooldown, you need to long press the screen. The ultimate transformation sound effect and animation will play, after which the evolved alien will appear.

Aliens which support ultimate transformations: Swampfire, Humangosaur, Spidermonkey, Big Chill, Echo Echo, Ghostfreak, Way Big, Cannonbolt, Wildmutt.

You can disable the Ultimate Transformation voices (eg. "Ultimate Swampfire!") using the Preferences Editor.

Omniverse

Activation sequence: R, R, R, L, R, L, R, L, R, L.

You can activate Omniverse by using it's activation sequence when in Ultimatrix mode.

To show the selector and start switching aliens you need to long press the screen. Once the selector is visible you must not take your finger of the screen.

To switch aliens to the right (clockwise) you need to drag your finger to the right side of the screen. To switch aliens to the left (counter-clockwise) you need to drag your finger to the left side of the screen.

Once you select the alien you want to transform into, bring your finger to the center of the screen and lift it of the screen. You will hear a beeping sound. To perform the actual transformation you need to tap the center of the screen one more time.

Omniverse contains one other sub-mode:

  • Randomizer Function

Randomizer Function

Activation sequence: R, L, L, L, R, R, L, L, L, R.

The Randomizer function can be activated when your Omnitrix is locked. After activating the Randomizer Function you will get a random alien about every 15 seconds. You can disable the Randomizer function by using the same sequence you used to activate it.

Life Form Lock

Activation sequence: L, L, L, R, R, R, L, L, L, L.

Omniverse transformations only last 10 seconds. However, you can stay transformed how long you want by activating Life Form Lock using the above mentioned activation sequence.

To activate Life Form Lock, first select an alien using the selector wheel. Once you have selected the alien and you start hearing the continous beeping sound you need to long press the screen. The screen content should shrink and now you can use the above mentioned activation sequence to activate Life Form Lock. You can return to the previous state (eg. cancel Life Form Lock activation) by long pressing the screen again.

When Life Form Lock is activated you won't transform back automatically until you remove the Life Form Lock cover. You can do that by dragging the cover (swiping on your screen) towards the top of the screen.

Bonus features

Voice Commands

Voice commands are currently available only in Prototype mode.

To activate voice commands you need to long press the right side of the screen. After doing that you will be prompted to say the phrase which identifies the voice command you want execute.

The following voice commands are currently available:

If you are having trouble getting Wear OS to recognize what you said you can use the feature that lets you edit the recognized text.

Color Customization

You can change the color theme of the Ultimatrix and Omniverse modes. You can choose between 12 different colors! To change colors you need to have the Alien Watch Companion app for Android and go into the settings screen. Available colors: dark blue, light blue, green (classic), lime, orange, yellow, pink, purple, cyan, grey, brown and red (albedo).

You can change the color theme of Ultimatrix and Omniverse using the Preferences Editor directly on your watch without the need to use the Companion app for Android.

Albedo

Activation sequence: R, R, L, R, R, L, L, R, L, L.

Albedo mode is a special color customization option which can't be selected in the Companion app. Albedo mode needs to be activated using the activation sequence above when in Ultimatrix or Omniverse mode.

Albedo mode has higher priority than the color theme selected using the Companion app and it will override it. However, you can return to the color theme selected in the Companion or the deafult color theme by disabling Albedo mode eg. using the same sequence you used to activate Albedo mode.

Screensaver

Screensaver or Ambient mode is the mode that's activated after you perform the palm gesture eg. cover your watch screen with your palm. The screen will become darker and a clock will appear on the screen. Depending on the mode which is currently active (Prototype, Ultimatrix or Omniverse) a different background will be used.

Currently there's no way to keep Alien Watch as a watch face, but this is a pretty good alternative.

Preferences Editor

Activation sequence: L, R, R, L, R, R, L, R, R, L.

You can activate the preferences editor in all modes. It allows you to quickly change your preferences (settings) directly on Wear OS, without the need to open the Alien Watch Companion app for Android. All of the above can be changed using the Preferences Editor.

Selector Speed

You can change the selector speed (the speed at which alien selection animations happen) for Ultimatrix mode and Omniverse mode.

Back Button Activation

A Prototype mode exclusive feature is the ability to require the back button (physical back button on your Wear OS device) to be pressed in order for the app to be activated.
