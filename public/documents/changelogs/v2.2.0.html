Fusion Transformations

You can now perform Fusion transformations like in Dr. Animo and the Mutant Ray. All three aliens seen in that episode are available: Heat jaws, Diamond matter and Stink arms.

Voice Commands

Prototype mode now supports voice input. You can use your voice to activate certain features instead of using their activation codes. You can find all available voice commands and how to use them on the official guide.

More Voice Sound Effects

In the previous update alien voice sound effects were added for Ultimate Transformations. With this update almost all aliens will scream their name (with a special animation) when performing Regular Transformations in Recalibrated mode.

Life Form Lock

Starting with this update Omniverse transformations are limited to only 10 seconds. In order to stay transformed longer you need to enable Life Form Lock. It's got a unique activation sequence which you can find out more about on the official guide.

Selector Speed Settings

You can now change the speed of the alien selection animations in Ultimatrix and Omniverse using the Preferences Editor.
